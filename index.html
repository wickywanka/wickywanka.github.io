#WHOAMI

I am an Information Security Student, a Cyber Security Researcher, whatever you wanna call me. The field of Cyber Security just amazes me every day.

I am currently doing my Bachelor’s in Computer Engineering at Vidyalankar Institute of Technology. I started my Cyber Security journey with Red Teaming – Penetration Testing, Web Security, Network Security. But Blue Teaming has piqued my curiosity to another level. I am fascinated by Detection & Response – Digital Forensics, Incident Response, Threat Intelligence, Malware Analysis, and Threat Hunting.

You can check out my projects as well. You can just visit my GitHub Profile

My interest was piqued in Cyber Security from my first year of undergrad, and since then I have never looked back. I have been constantly developing my skills, exploring new areas, and continuing to learn something new. I practice my skills on platforms like tryhackme.com, hackthebox.com, rangeforce.com, and any of the available free platforms out there. I successfully achieved the EJPT (Junior Penetration Tester), CompTIA Security+, Fortinet NSE 1, and NSE 2, along with Microsoft Certified Security, Compliance, and Identity Fundamentals along with Certified Ethical Hacker Practical (not the Theory) to prove my knowledge in the field. Also, I have done courses from Coursera and Udemy in the same.

Participation in Hackathons and CTFs is also a part that I totally enjoy. I have successfully won 2 hackathons – Institute Level and National Level, and I led both of them. I achieved the 34th Rank in SANS South Asia CTF and a position among the top 100 in HackerEarth CTF. I also participated in a National Level Engineering Olympiad in which I secured an All India Rank of 99. Me and my team were also selected as Finalists for Smart India Hackathon 2022, but unfortunately we did not win. But it was an awesome offline Hackathon experience, as the earlier hackathons I participated in were all online due to COVID.

I have done multiple internships in the field along with some volunteering positions as well. You can check all of that here

I have authored publications as well. My papers have been published in IEEE, International Journals, Elsevier and I have also contributed in writing white papers for helping the organizations as well.

I am passionate about giving back to the community and helping others get into this field. That’s why I am a part of many non-profit open-source communities – GDSC, OWASP, Null, and Cyversity. I am currently volunteering at Null – The Open Security Community as a Research Volunteer where I am a part of the Crypto Team and we are writing a white paper that describes all the cryptographic vulnerabilities from 2021. I occasionally volunteer as a Judge at the Global OSINT Search Party organized by Trace Labs. Also, I was a Technical Core Member at Google Developer Student’s Club VIT Mumbai.

I love attending security conferences, workshops, events and have attended conferences by DEFCON, SANS, Bsides, NullCon, and many more. I have also been a competitive-level swimmer(State-Level) and gymnast(District-Level) . I love to play Harmonium and Tabla in my free time and have demonstrated my skills via exams. Doing Calisthenics is also a part of my daily routine nowadays. I am an avid gamer and play all kinds of games – from story-like single-player to competitive multi-player. Photography and Trekking are also a part of my keen interests.

Work

DigiSec360 (Security Analyst Intern)

Performed hands-on penetration tests on Web Applications, Cloud, Active Directory, APIs, as well as on-site OT Network Infrastructures, and provided a thorough security assessment of client applications by creating well-represented reports based on the OWASP Top 10 and SANS 25 guidelines leveraging a plethora of open-source and commercial tools. - Implemented a workflow to evaluate the client assets by utilizing numerous open-source tools and techniques and by working in collaboration with the rest of the team. - Evaluated and Triaged threats by rating them on an ordinal scale with the help of the DREAD Framework. - Designed a comprehensive checklist for API Security. - Researched and implemented a methodology for secure code review using SAST, DAST, and SCA tools. - Responsible for recommending mitigation of risks and vulnerabilities and further hardening the policies and rules of the clients and automating the manual tasks with scripting languages.

Null - The Open Security Community (Research Volunteer)

We are evaluating and investigating the cryptographic vulnerabilities caused in the year 2021, and volunteering to write a white paper on the same, along with recruiting new talent and spreading awareness of the community.

Cyberfrat (Project Intern)

I collaborated and led the project “Infosec Fusion” with my team, where we explored and assessed Security Architecture and Engineering, Communication and Network Security, and IAM. Authored on giving security advisory services for clients, which resulted in an efficient and secured workflow in their organization while referencing the NIST CSF. Evaluate the risk and value of business processes and document key business process steps, supporting technologies, and inventory structured and unstructured information assets. Planned, and designed healthy security architectures for the clients, and achieved the title of “Intern of the Month” for August.

Haryana Police (Summer Intern)

Gained insights on DFIR, Cryptography, Blockchain, Cloud Security, Web-app Pentesting, Malware Analysis, and many more Infosec-related topics from the targeted sessions by cybersecurity experts. - Witnessed side-effects of not following proper cyber hygiene and unintended miss-use of cyberspace with nerve-wracking case studies on the job. Became aware of distinct Cyber Laws in the Indian constitution and how to report them if witnessed.

Virtually Testing Foundation

Throughout the internship, I was able to learn, gain knowledge, and had hands-on experience about the Foundations of Cybersecurity. Managing an internship program with 200+interns and 3 staff members. Organizing daily meetings with staff to ensure smooth working of internships and projects.
